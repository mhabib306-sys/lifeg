Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Go to Settings and enter a GitHub Personal Access Token. Your data will automatically sync to a private GitHub repository.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings, scroll down, and tap "Export Data". This downloads a JSON file with all your tasks, tracking data, and settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes your authentication credentials and all local data.

Can I use Homebase offline?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. Changes sync to the cloud when you're back online.

How do I connect Google Calendar?

Go to Settings > Integrations and tap "Connect Google Calendar". You'll be prompted to sign in with your Google account.
